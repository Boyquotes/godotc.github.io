### Dot Product for Projection
![](attachments/Pasted%20image%2020220821143607.png)
$$ \begin{align}
\vec{b}_\perp=k\widehat{a}\\
k\widehat{a}= cos\theta\ \|\vec{b}\| \\
||\vec{b}||cos\theta=\frac{\vec{a}*\vec{b}}{||\vec{b}||}\\
\\

k=k\widehat{a}


\end{align}
$$

- $\widehat{a}$ is a normalization vector.
